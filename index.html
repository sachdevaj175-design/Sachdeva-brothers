Our Process

From Raw Material to
Ready-to-Use

Our vertically integrated manufacturing process ensures complete quality control at every stage, from raw copper drawing to final testing.

01
Drawing

Raw copper rods drawn into precise wire gauges

02
Bunching

Multiple strands bunched together for flexibility

03
Tin Plating

Hot-dip tin plating for corrosion protection

04
Insulation

PVC, XLPE or PTFE insulation extrusion applied

05
Coring

Central core formation for multi-core cables

06
Laying

Precise laying of cores in designed patterns

07
Shielding

Braided or foil shields for EMI protection

08
Taping

Protective tape wrapping between layers

09
Jacketing

Outer jacket extrusion for final protection

10
Harnessing

11
Moulding

12
Testing
